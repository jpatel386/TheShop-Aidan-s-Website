things to do at the shop

We're bang in the middle of the JCR. Dead easy to find.

  • Live Lounge

    Every year at Christmas and Easter, we hold our "Live Lounge". The idea (by Alex Tarrant-Anderson) is a revamped version of "Jazz Rock & Cocktails". Acts from all over Durham perform including our own Aidanites. Message our events officer to find out more, and maybe you can perform too.

  • Toastie of the Week (TOTW)

    Every week we have our own Toastie of the Week. Similar to Toastie Favourites, it's a special pre-made toastie featuring on the infamous blackboard for everyone to see and order. Submit your own toastie for next week, and it could win!

  • Games and Films!

    We have a wide range of games across the PS3 and the Wii U including the latest FIFA and MarioKart etc. Come down and beat the fastest lap times. Lookout for the MarioKart competition. Contact our Events Officer for more info. In addition to this we have plenty of DVDs for you to watch in the bailey room on the big screen. Or if you fancy a quiet night with a board game, we have a ton of those too. See in store for a full list.

  • Chat to Us!

    It gets quite lonely sometimes on shift. Procrastinate a bit and have a chat with us. We've got bar stools so you can sit comfortably too!
